Main Speakers, Workshops, and Other Activities

The primary purpose of the Funcoast Area Convention of Narcotics Anonymous (FACNA) is to provide a special celebration of our recovery, a reflection of our diversity, which demonstrates our common bond. Main speakers, workshops, and various activities are included to encourage greater unity and fellowship amongst our members.
